FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes

What industries do you specialize in?

CyberShades has deep expertise across multiple industries including finance, healthcare, retail, manufacturing, and logistics. Our diverse experience allows us to bring cross-industry insights and best practices to every project, while also understanding the specific challenges and regulations of your sector.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope, complexity, and requirements. A simple web application might take 2-3 months, while a complex enterprise solution could take 6-12 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific project needs.

What development methodology do you follow?

We primarily use Agile methodologies like Scrum and Kanban, which allow for iterative development, regular client feedback, and flexibility to adapt to changing requirements. For certain projects, we may also incorporate elements of traditional waterfall or hybrid approaches based on the specific needs.

Do you provide post-launch support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-launch support and maintenance services. Our support plans include regular updates, performance monitoring, security patches, and technical assistance. We also provide enhancement services to help your solution evolve with your business needs.

How do you ensure the security of the solutions you develop?

Security is integrated throughout our development process. We conduct regular code reviews, implement secure coding practices, perform penetration testing, and utilize automated security scanning tools. Our solutions comply with industry standards like OWASP, and we can implement specific security measures based on your regulatory requirements.
