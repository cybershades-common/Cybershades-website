Our Journey

The CyberShades Story

From humble beginnings to becoming Jaipur's leading IT service provider, our journey has been marked by innovation, growth, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

2017

The Foundation

CyberShades was founded with a vision to bridge the technology gap for businesses in Rajasthan. Starting with just 3 passionate developers in a small office in C-Scheme, Jaipur.

Genesis & Vision
2018

First Major Breakthrough

Secured our first enterprise client and delivered a comprehensive ERP solution. This success established our reputation for handling complex business challenges.

Enterprise Success
2019

Expanding Horizons

Expanded our team to 15 members and ventured into cloud computing and AI/ML solutions. Opened partnerships with major technology providers.

Growth & Innovation
2020

Digital Transformation Leader

Despite global challenges, we emerged as a leader in digital transformation, helping businesses adapt to remote work and digital-first approaches.

Resilience & Leadership
2021

Innovation & Research

Launched our AI/ML division and IoT solutions. Reached 100+ successful projects and established our research & development center.

AI & Innovation
2022

Market Leadership

Became one of the top 5 IT service providers in Rajasthan. Expanded our team to 35+ professionals and opened our second office location.

Market Dominance
2023

Excellence Recognition

Received multiple industry awards for innovation and customer satisfaction. Established partnerships with global technology leaders.

Awards & Recognition
2024

Scaling New Heights

Reached 50+ team members and 250+ completed projects. Launched our cybersecurity division and expanded into enterprise AI solutions.

Scale & Security
2025

Future Ready

Continuing our mission to empower businesses with next-generation technology solutions. Planning international expansion and advanced R&D initiatives.

Future Vision
